Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have about our platform.

How long is the free trial?

Our free trial lasts 14 days with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods work?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately or at next billing cycle.

How many users can I add?

Depends on your plan: Starter (5), Pro (25), Business (100), Enterprise (unlimited).

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption, are SOC 2 certified, and GDPR compliant.

What support do you offer?

Email support for all plans, live chat for Pro+, and dedicated account manager for Enterprise.
