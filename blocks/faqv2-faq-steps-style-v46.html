How It Works

Common questions about getting started with our platform.

1

Click the "Sign Up" button, enter your email address, and create a password. You'll receive a verification email - click the link to activate your account and start using the platform.

2

After logging in, click "New Project" on your dashboard. Give it a name, choose a template or start from scratch, and invite your team members. Your project will be ready in seconds.

3

Go to Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter their email addresses and select their roles (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). They'll receive an invitation email to join your workspace.

4

Navigate to Settings → Integrations. Browse our 100+ available integrations, click "Connect" on the ones you need, and follow the authorization steps. Most integrations are set up in under a minute.

5

Click the help icon in the bottom right corner to access our help center, search documentation, or start a live chat with our support team. Pro users also have access to email and phone support.
