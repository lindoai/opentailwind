Orders & Shipping

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Click the link or enter the number on our tracking page to see real-time updates.

We offer Standard (5-7 days), Express (2-3 days), and Next Day delivery. Free shipping on orders over $50.

Address changes are possible before your order ships. Contact us immediately through live chat or email.
