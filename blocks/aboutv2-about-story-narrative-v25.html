It started with a simple question: Why is collaboration so hard?

In 2016, we were a team of three working at different companies, frustrated by the same problem. Every tool we used felt like it was designed for a different era. Email threads that went nowhere. Meetings that could have been messages. Documents scattered across a dozen platforms.

So we quit our jobs, pooled our savings, and started building. The first version was rough—really rough. But it worked. And more importantly, people loved it. Within six months, we had 1,000 users. Within a year, 50,000.

"We didn't set out to build a billion-dollar company. We just wanted to solve a problem that drove us crazy every day."

— Alex Rivera, Co-founder

Today, we're a team of 500+ people across 12 countries, serving millions of users. But we've never forgotten those early days in a cramped apartment, fueled by coffee and the belief that we could make work better for everyone.

That's still what drives us. Every feature we ship, every hire we make, every decision we take—it all comes back to that original question. And we're just getting started.

Alex Rivera

Co-founder & CEO

Written on the company's 8th anniversary
