Tech support center

Quick answers to help you troubleshoot and get back on track.

How do I set up my device?

Follow our quick start guide to connect your device. Power on, download the app, and pair via Bluetooth or WiFi in under 5 minutes.

Why won't my device connect?

Check your WiFi signal strength and ensure you're within range. Try restarting both your router and device, then reconnect.

How do I update firmware?

Open the app, go to Settings > Device > Check for Updates. Keep your device plugged in during the update process.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email and follow the secure link to create a new password.

Is my data secure?

We use end-to-end encryption and never sell your data. All information is stored on secure servers with regular audits.

How do I contact support?

Reach us via live chat in the app, email, or call our 24/7 helpline. Average response time is under 2 hours.
