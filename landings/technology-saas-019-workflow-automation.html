Security Center

Questions & answers

How is my data encrypted?

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Your encryption keys are stored in hardware security modules.

What compliance certifications do you have?

We maintain SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance. Annual third-party audits verify our security controls.

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Go to Security Settings and click "Enable 2FA." Choose your preferred method: authenticator app, SMS, or hardware key.

How can I view my security audit logs?

Access detailed audit logs from the Admin Console under Security → Audit Logs. Filter by date, user, action type, or IP address.
