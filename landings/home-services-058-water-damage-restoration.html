Travel FAQ

Most Travelers Ask About

Quick answers to common questions about bookings, cancellations, and travel policies.

Log into your account and go to "My Trips" to view your bookings. Select the booking you want to change and click "Modify" or "Cancel." Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before departure.

Our prices include all taxes and fees with no hidden charges. Depending on your package, accommodations, transfers, and selected activities are included.

We strongly recommend travel insurance for all trips. It covers unexpected cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost luggage. Add coverage during checkout.

Your confirmation email is sent immediately after booking. It includes your itinerary, booking reference, and all important details. Also accessible in "My Trips."

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Installment payment plans available with no interest if paid within 6 months.
