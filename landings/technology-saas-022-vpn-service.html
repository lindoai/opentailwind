Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Click the link or enter the number on our tracking page to see real-time delivery updates.

What's your return policy?

We offer 30-day hassle-free returns on all items. Simply initiate a return from your order history and print the prepaid label.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and buy-now-pay-later options like Klarna and Afterpay.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! Select the gift wrap option at checkout for $4.99. We'll beautifully wrap your item and include a personalized message card.
