Questions About Buying & Selling

Expert answers to help you navigate the real estate market with confidence.

How do I start the home buying process?

Begin by getting pre-approved for a mortgage to understand your budget. Then, work with one of our agents to identify properties that match your criteria and schedule viewings.

What costs should I expect?

Budget for closing costs (2-5% of loan), inspection fees, appraisal costs, and moving expenses.

How long does selling take?

On average, homes sell within 30-60 days. From listing to closing typically takes 60-90 days.

What should I do to prepare my home for sale?

Focus on decluttering, deep cleaning, and minor repairs. Consider fresh paint in neutral colors and enhancing curb appeal. Our staging consultation service can help maximize your home's appeal.
